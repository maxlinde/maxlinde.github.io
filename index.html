About me

I am a PhD student at the Psychometrics & Statistics group at the faculty of Behvaioural & Social Sciences at the University of Groningen.

In my PhD project, I am developing ease-to-use Bayesian statistical techniques and software for research in the biomedical sciences (but also other fields). A major focus lies on reducing complexity and increasing user-friendliness, in order to reach a wide audience of researchers.
